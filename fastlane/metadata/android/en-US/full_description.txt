SuperX is a powerful and private video downloader for Android, built with freedom and flexibility in mind. It allows you to save videos from a wide variety of websites and platforms directly to your device for offline viewing. As a fully open-source application, it contains no trackers, no advertisements, and all processing is done locally on your device to protect your privacy.

<b>Core Features:</b>

*   <b>Wide Compatibility:</b> Download videos from many websites and social media platforms.
*   <b>Advanced Stream Support:</b> Fully supports modern streaming protocols, including HLS (.m3u8) and DASH (.mpd) manifests, as well as standard MP4 streams and live broadcasts.
*   <b>Standard Format:</b> All downloaded videos are saved in the universal .mp4 container for maximum compatibility with any player.
*   <b>Client-Side Processing:</b> All operations happen on your device. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring your activity remains private.

<b>Advanced Privacy & Networking:</b>

*   <b>Cookie Support:</b> Allows you to use your browser's cookies to access and download videos from private or login-protected areas.
*   <b>Full Proxy Integration:</b> Enhance your privacy and bypass network restrictions with built-in support for HTTP and SOCKS proxies.
*   <b>Proxy Chaining & DNS Security:</b> For ultimate privacy, the app supports proxy chains and can be configured with encrypted DNS (DoH/DoT) and custom servers.

SuperX is the ideal tool for users who demand control over their media and their privacy. By providing direct access to download streams and robust networking tools, it ensures a free and open way to access online video content.
